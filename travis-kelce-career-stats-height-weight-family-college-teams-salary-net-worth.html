Travis Kelce: Career stats, height, weight, family, college, teams, salary, net worth

 Posted on July 27, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  441 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Born on October 5th, 1989, in Westlake, Ohio, Travis Kelce has etched his name in the annals of NFL history as a dominant tight end. His journey is a testament to sheer talent, unwavering determination, and an infectious passion for the game.

Professional Career Stats:

Kelce’s professional career has been nothing short of extraordinary. Drafted by the Kansas City Chiefs in the third round of the 2013 NFL Draft, he has since become an integral part of the team’s success, amassing impressive statistics:

7-time Pro Bowl selection (2015-2021)

4-time First-team All-Pro (2016, 2018, 2020, 2022)

2-time Super Bowl Champion (LIV, LVII)

Super Bowl LVII Receiving Touchdown

NFL record for most consecutive seasons with 1,000 yards receiving by a tight end (7)

NFL record for most receiving yards by a tight end in a single season (1,416 in 2020)

Member of the NFL 2010s All-Decade Team

Height and Weight:

Standing at a towering 6 feet 5 inches and weighing 260 pounds, Kelce possesses the ideal size and athleticism for a dominant tight end. His frame allows him to effectively box out defenders, shield the quarterback, and make acrobatic catches in traffic.

College, Draft, and Teams Played:

Kelce began his collegiate career at Cincinnati Bearcats, where he honed his skills and emerged as a standout tight end. In 2013, he entered the NFL Draft and was selected by the Kansas City Chiefs, where he has remained and flourished ever since.

Jersey Number:

Kelce is easily recognizable on the field by his signature jersey number 87, a number that has become synonymous with his talent and infectious energy.

Super Bowl Wins:

Kelce has tasted the ultimate glory of the Super Bowl twice, capturing championships in Super Bowl LIV and LVII. In Super Bowl LVII, he cemented his legacy by scoring a crucial touchdown, further solidifying his status as a Super Bowl champion.

Religion and Ethnicity:

Kelce proudly identifies as Christian. His faith plays a significant role in shaping his character and provides him with unwavering support and strength.

Family and Siblings:

Kelce maintains a close relationship with his family, who have been a constant source of support and inspiration. He is the older brother of Philadelphia Eagles center Jason Kelce, and their brotherly bond has become a popular topic in the NFL world.

Wife or Girlfriend and Children:

Kelce is currently engaged to Kayla Nicole, and the couple welcomed their first child, a daughter, in April 2023.

Weekly and Annual Salary:

Kelce’s dedication and talent are reflected in his current weekly salary of $82,692.31 and an annual salary of $4,270,240.31.

Net Worth:

Estimates place Kelce’s net worth between $15 million and $20 million.
